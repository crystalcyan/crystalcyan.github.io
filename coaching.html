Coaching

For individuals at all levels of experience looking to level up in their careers, I offer 1:1 coaching focused on topics such as: job search (resume review, mock interviews), developing technical skills and leadership and influence skills (in product management, design, and UX research), and overall career development (developing a professional development plan).

For senior individual contributors and people managers, I offer 1:1 coaching and small group coaching (by application only).

In addition to my independent coaching practice, I occasionally coach in partnership with organizations, such as:

- First Round Capital (1:1 coaching for senior product managers)

- Within (group coaching for senior designers, Design Managers, and Directors)

- School of Visual Concepts (SVC) (1:1 coaching for entry level designers)

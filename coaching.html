Coaching

For individuals at all levels of experience looking to grow, I offer 1:1 career coaching for topics such as: transitioning to a new role, preparing for a promotion, and making the most of 1:1s with your manager. For founders and leaders, I offer 1:1 leadership coaching for topics such as: battling imposter syndrome, navigating difficult people management situations, and getting out of analysis paralysis and into taking action.

I've coached:

- Entrepreneurs in Sahil Lavingia's Minimalist Entrepreneur community

- UX Design students at the Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA) & School of Visual Concepts (SVC)

- Mid-Level & Senior Product Managers & Designers at organizations such as Facebook, Google, Dropbox, Expedia, Alaska Airlines, PatientPing

- Directors and Managers at organizations such as Facebook, Adobe, Gates Foundation, Accenture, Abstract, eBay

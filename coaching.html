Coaching

For individuals at all levels of experience looking to grow in their careers, I offer 1:1 career coaching for topics such as: transitioning to a new role, preparing for a promotion or stretch assignment/project by developing specific leadership and strategy skills, and developing a plan for professional development plan and making the most of 1:1s with your manager.

For senior individual contributors and people managers, I offer small group coaching (subscribe be notified when applications open) and 1:1 leadership coaching for for topics such as: managing people and navigating difficult conversations with direct reports, leading a team's strategy (storytelling vision) and operations (developing processes), contributing effectively and working cross-functionally with your leadership team, and managing up with your executive.

Win the Offer

2 session resume/portfolio review and mock interview package. I recommend this for those who are actively interviewing. Make the Leap

2 month career coaching package. I recommend this for those looking to transition from another function or industry into product management, product design, or UX research. We'll partner on planning your job search, preparing for interviews, and negotiating offers.

Summit

3 month career coaching package. I recommend this for those who are early in their career in product management, product design, or UX research. I'll offer candid feedback on what skills you can focus on developing further, and we'll partner on developing a professional development plan and putting it into action.

Level Up

6 month leadership coaching package. I recommend this for those preparing for a promotion and/or those looking to sharpen skills while leading a challenging project. We'll partner on developing a professional development plan and putting it into action, creating visibility for your achievements, and effectively managing up to strengthen your relationship with your manager.

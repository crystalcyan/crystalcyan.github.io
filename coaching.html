Coaching

For individuals at all levels of experience looking to grow in their careers, I offer 1:1 career coaching focused on topics such as: transitioning to a new role, preparing for a promotion or stretch assignment/project by developing specific leadership and strategy skills, and developing a plan for professional development plan and making the most of 1:1s with your manager.

For senior individual contributors and people managers, I offer 1:1 leadership coaching and small group coaching (by application only).
